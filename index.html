MR GRADE PLYWOODS

We are one of the prominent MR Grade Plywood Manufacturer and Supplier in Yamuna Nagar, Haryana. We fabricate MR Grade Plywood from finest wood by using the latest technologies. In addition, our fine MR Grade Plywood is appreciated by the clients for its unbeatable features and cost- efficiency. We offer MR Grade Plywood in different sizes and specifications.

BWR GRAD PLYWOODS

BWP Grade boards are manufactured from selected and seasoned timber battens. These are bonded with Phenol Formaldehyde resin for BWP grade. The block boards are resistant to twisting/warping and have superb screw holding & nail holding capability. This is mainly possible because of seasoned & durable timber which is used. These battens are systematically arranged and utmost care is taken to avoid any extra gap between the battens. Regular test are conducted to maintain the quality of block boards as per the Indian & International standards.

MARIN PLYWOODS

Marine Plywood Manufactured at P.R. Industries is specially treated plywood that is designed to resist rotting in a high moisture environment. It is bonded with undiluted Phenol Formaldehyde Resin at extremely high temperature and pressure. Therefore, this plywood can take extreme changes in temperature, humidity and alternate wetting and drying which are all the prerequisites for plywood used in marine applications. Being powder proof it guarantees protection against borer & termites. This marine plywood is strong enough to withstand excess load, thrust and vibrations. It is dimensionally stable and stronger than the others.

FIRE RETARDENT PLYWOOD

To meet diverse requirements of our valued clients, we are engaged in offering an excellent range of Fire Retardant Wood Panel Products that are absolutely non-hygroscopic. Our offered plywood is manufactured by our proficient professionals using high grade raw material and innovative technology in line with industry norms. Products are availed in variegated sizes and designs as per the needs of our prestigious clients. Our quality experts test these products on different parameters so as to maintain its quality.
